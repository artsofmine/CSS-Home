The Best Toast Ever

Ingredient Amount
Bread 2 pieces
Butter 1-2 slice American cheese
Salami 4-8 slices
Butter 1,5 tablespoons
  1. Step 1. Thinly spread one side of each bread slice with butter
  2. Step 2. Prepare the salami by taking 2-3 slices and rolling them up like a cigar, slice as you would cut bread and you get salami strings, use your fingers to break apart as you put on the sandwich
  3. Step 3. Top with American cheese and place the top piece of bread
  4. Step 4. Using a pan over medium heat, squirt a little cooking oil and place sandwich in pan giving it a little circular motion to evenly spread the oil and butter
  5. Step 5. Cook until golden brown checking the underside with a spatula from time to time
  6. Step 6. Flip the sandwich and push down gently on the bread with spatula, cover and cook an addition minute or so
  7. Step 7. Slide it out, slice it up, enjoy!
